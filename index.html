About Time4You:

Time4You has been created by students of EBS University, Germany. Our aim is to help young people in the Rheingau region to find flexible jobs that they can perform whenever they want and can while supporting the elderly people in the region. How is that possible? On our platform, nursing homes and social agencies can communicate times where young people can read to the elderly, go on a walk or play games together. Young people get paid and our society grows together. We hope you find your place and the Time4You and your region.
